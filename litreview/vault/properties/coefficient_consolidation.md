## Derivation
cv = k/(mv*gamma_w) = k*(1+e0)/(av*gamma_w). Buildable from USCS-keyed k (permeability) and Cc/mv, but it is a deterministic combination of permeability + compressibility, both already USCS-keyed.  [Terzaghi 1D consolidation]
