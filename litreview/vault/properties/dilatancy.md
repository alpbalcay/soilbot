## Derivation
psi = phi' - phi'_cv (Bolton 1986: psi proportional to relative dilatancy index IR), needs Dr/relative density which is SPT-bound. Non-SPT version is a USCS-typical psi constant (dense sand ~5-15 deg, clay ~0).  [Bolton (1986)]

## Notes
Real dilatancy needs density state (SPT/CPT). Non-SPT form is a USCS constant. Low influence (68) and redundant; excluded.
