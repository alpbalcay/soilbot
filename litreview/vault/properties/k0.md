# k0

*Also called:* Coefficient of earth pressure at rest; coefficient of earth pressure at rest; Earth pressure coefficient at rest; In situ horizontal stress coefficient (at-rest earth pressure coefficient)

## Derivation
Jaky NC: K0 = 1 - sin(phi'), with phi' from a USCS-typical drained friction angle (SW/SP ~34-36 -> K0~0.42-0.44; CL ~28 -> K0~0.53; CH ~22 -> K0~0.63). If an OCR estimate existed, K0_OC = (1-sin phi')*OCR^sin(phi') (Mayne & Kulhawy), but use the NC form given no reliable OCR.  [Jaky (1944); Mayne & Kulhawy (1982)]
