## Derivation
Engine emits phi_peck_deg = 27.1 + 0.3*(N1)60 - 0.00054*(N1)60^2 (Peck-Hanson-Thornburn) and phi_hatanaka_deg = 3.5*sqrt(N60)+22.3, both gated to granular USCS.
