# shear modulus

**Category:** dynamic · **Influence (citation-weighted):** 214.69 across 33 papers

**Status:** 🆕 not yet derived · 🟢 non-leaky · 🔧 derivable from our inputs

*Also called:* Shear modulus; Small-strain shear modulus; Maximum (small-strain) shear modulus; Normalized shear modulus / modulus reduction ratio

## Derivation
Small-strain Gmax = rho*Vs^2 with Vs from a USCS+stress correlation, or empirical Gmax = A*(OCR^k)*sigma'_m^n / (0.3+0.7e^2) (Hardin & Black). Without Vs, e, or OCR it reduces to Gmax ~ f(sigma'_v0) with USCS constants.  [Hardin & Black (1968); Seed & Idriss (1970)]

## Notes
Only derivable through assumed Vs/e (themselves USCS constants) and the existing sigma'. Largely redundant with effective_stress + USCS. Low marginal info; not shortlisted.
