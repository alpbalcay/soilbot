# shear wave velocity

**Category:** dynamic · **Influence (citation-weighted):** 89.4 across 13 papers

**Status:** 🆕 not yet derived · 🟢 non-leaky · 🔧 derivable from our inputs

*Also called:* Shear wave velocity; Shear-wave velocity; Shear-wave velocity (overburden-corrected); Shear-wave velocity (stress-corrected)

## Derivation
Vs = a*sigma'_v0^b with USCS-typical a,b, or Vs from N60 (Vs=97*N60^0.314, leaky). Non-SPT route is only Vs ~ f(sigma'_v0, USCS-constant) which duplicates effective_stress+USCS.  [Andrus & Stokoe; Ohta & Goto (1978)]

## Notes
Non-SPT form is just sigma' raised to a USCS-keyed power -> highly collinear with existing sigma_eff. Low marginal info; not shortlisted.
