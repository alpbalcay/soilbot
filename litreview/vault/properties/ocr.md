## Derivation
Only as a depth/geology heuristic: OCR = sigma'_p / sigma'_v0 with sigma'_p estimated from a constant crust preconsolidation or a regional unloading offset; or Mayne's OCR=0.5*(N60/sigma'_v0)... (that is SPT). Non-SPT route requires assuming a geologic preconsolidation profile not available per-interval in NJ.  [Mayne (2007) for SPT/CPT; Mesri for crust]
